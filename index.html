ABOUT THE SHOP

Visit Baguio City's remarkable and phenomenal boutique and cafe shop in the northern Philippines. Drop by Lorem's Bookcafe & Boutique to relax and have some peace of mind while indulging yourself to luxury. Dine and ease up in comfort and style at the Bookcafe on the breezy 2nd floor terrace. The Boutique store on the ground floor has everything a fashionista needs.



ABOUT THE OWNER

Ghearrina Lorem Fajardo grew up in San Fernando City, La Union suburb, surrounded by dazzling outfits and pile of makeups. At the age of 7, she already knows how to work with beauty products and chooses her own OOTDs. She loves the aroma of coffee with 2 teaspoons of creamer and a ginormous amount of sugar. She is now 16 years old, an incoming Grade 11 in the next school year. She is also known to be a flexible dancer and a dreamy singer. She is one of the choir of angels of God, serving at the Cathedral of Saint William the Hermit Parish. She believes in the saying that "Beauty is in the eye of the beholder."
